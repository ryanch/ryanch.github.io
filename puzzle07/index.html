Detailed Rules

How to Play

In this puzzle hunt you are given a series of puzzles to solve. Part of the challenge is that each of the puzzles have little or no direction, part of solving the puzzle is figuring what the puzzle is about.

Rules

  1. To win, submit the answer to each puzzle on this page using the submission directions below.
  2. The winning team is the team who completes all puzzles first. Tie breaker is most puzzles solved.
  3. Submit your answers before 4:25pm EST using the Submission directions below.
  4. You may use any resource or reference (including Google) for this puzzle. However, you may not use participants from other teams.
  5. You may solve the puzzles in any order.
  6. These rules are not a clue or a part of any of the puzzles.
  7. Hints are unlimited. To ask for a hint use the "Ask for help" button in zoom, somoene will join the room to help you.
  8. Hint: In this puzzle hunt you will NEVER need to convert a jumble of letters like "renge" into the word "green". If you find a jumble of letters, you are missing a step to the puzzle which will provide the sequence.
  9. Have fun!
